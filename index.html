about us

what makes our coffee special?

Java Lava is a charming café located in Perth, Western Australia. We specialize in serving exceptional coffee, delicious beverages, and delectable food. Our passionate baristas craft each cup of coffee with care, using carefully sourced beans. We offer a variety of beverages, including teas, juices, hot chocolates, and smoothies. Our menu features a range of breakfast, lunch, and dessert options made with locally sourced ingredients.

The cozy ambiance and friendly staff create a welcoming atmosphere for all visitors. Come and experience the magic of Java Lava, where passion and perfection meet in every sip and bite.
